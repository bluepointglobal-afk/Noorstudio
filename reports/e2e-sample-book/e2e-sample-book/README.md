# NoorStudio E2E Sample Book

**Title:** *Amina and the Masjid Mystery*

**Premise:** A 9-year-old Muslim girl detective (Amina), wearing hijab, solves a gentle mystery at her local masjid with good adab.

## Outputs
- `Amina_and_the_Masjid_Mystery_KDP.pdf` — print-ready interior + cover (single PDF for demo).
- `screenshots/` — key screenshots from the generation process.

## Notes on Islamic content guidelines
- No depiction of Prophets.
- Respectful masjid etiquette (adab), cleanliness, quiet voices.
- No superstition/black magic; mystery resolved with practical reasoning.
- Includes simple du'a and polite Islamic phrases (e.g., *Bismillah*, *Alhamdulillah*), kept age-appropriate.
